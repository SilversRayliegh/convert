SENTENCES

Lesson 7: Correcting Fragments and Run-ons (pp. 32–35)

A fragment is an incomplete sentence. A run-on sentence is two complete sentences that run together.

Make each fragment a complete sentence by matching it with the correct subject or predicate. Write the letter of the words you choose on the line.

  • 1.Pine cones
  • 2.helped me find a book.
  • 3.move tons of dirt.
  • The librarian
  • grow on pine trees.
  • The big bulldozers

Write each run-on sentence as a compound sentence.

Write each run-on sentence as two separate sentences.
