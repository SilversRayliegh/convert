Lesson 2:
Statements and Questions (pp. 12–15)

A statement tells something. A statement ends with a period (.).
A question asks something. A question ends with a question mark (?).

Add a period (.) to end each statement. Add a question mark (?) to end each question.
  • Do you have a favorite sport
  • I love soccer games
  • My father works on Saturdays
  • Can you play an instrument
  • Five goldfish swim in the tank
  • What is your dog’s name
  • Where do you want to go
  • The girls run to the playgroun
  • Vo draws a picture of his family
  • The hikers go up the trail
  • Who knows the rules of the game
  • The sun shines brightly today
  • Which color do you like
  • Can he come with us
  • The boys are tired tonight
