NOUNS

Lesson 9: Nouns (pp. 44–47)

A noun is a word that names a person, place, or thing.

Find the noun or nouns in each sentence. Write the noun or nouns on the line.

  • Many leaves fell from the trees.
  • The boys rode their bikes down the lane.
  • The lemonade is sweet.
  • Some new people moved to our neighborhood.
  • My uncle buys some fruit at the store.
  • The clown is the star of the show.
  • We held a fair in our town.
  • The sailor checked his large map.
  • The chipmunk raced across the path.
  • The city is very noisy.
  • Pickles are good with sandwiches.
  • The trucks drove slowly over the bridge.
  • Helmets provide safety against injuries.
  • My brother took pictures at the concert.
  • The seals swam through the water.
