Lesson 19: Simple-Tense: Future (pp. 88–91)

Present-tense verbs tell about actions that happen now. Past-tense verbs tell about actions that already happened. Future tense verbs tell about actions that are going to happen.
Use the verb will with the main verb to form the future tense. Shall is sometimes used when the subject is I or we.
