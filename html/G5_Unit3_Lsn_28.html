Lesson 28: Verbs Often Misused (pp. 124–127)

Verbs that have meanings that are related or similar can cause confusion. These include teach and learn, sit and set, and let and leave. Study the meaning of these words so that you use them correctly.
     teach—to give knowledge
     learn—to get knowledge
     sit—to rest
     set—to put or place; to establish
     let—to allow or permit
     leave—to go away from; to let remain in place
