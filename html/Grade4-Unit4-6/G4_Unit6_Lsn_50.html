Lesson 50: Words Often Misspelled (pp. 48–51)

Homophones are words that sound the same but have different spellings and meanings. Some homophones are possessive pronouns, such as their, and others are contractions, such as they’re. Think about the meaning of the word to help you choose the correct spelling.
