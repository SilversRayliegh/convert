Lesson 15: Past-Tense Verbs (pp. 72–75)

A verb in the past tense tells about an action that already happened.

Write the past-tense form of the verb in parentheses to correctly complete each sentence.

  • Justin to my e-mail. (reply)
  • My mom muffins for the whole class. (bake)
  • The popcorn inside the big pot. (pop)
  • The train conductor tickets from the passengers. (collect)
  • The key the treasure chest. (unlock)
  • The children the vocabulary words. (study)
  • The audience along with the music. (hum)
  • Tania the phone number. (remember)
  • The cats at the television screen. (stare)
  • The shoppers plastic bags. (use)
  • We the fish in a large pan. (fry)
  • Omar the paper into little pieces. (rip)
  • I my coat on my way out the door. (grab)
  • The team for good weather. (hope)
  • The lights on and off. (blink)
