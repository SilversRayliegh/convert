Read the letter below. There are mistakes in the use of capital letters, commas, end marks, and quotation marks. There are also mistakes in spelling and the use of homophones. Find the 12 mistakes, and use the proofreading marks to correct them.

 330 Henderson Drive

 Stillwater MN 55082

 september 3, 2013

Dear Marie,

     We had the best vacation ever. We went to Yellowstone

National Park. The trip was amazeing! The best part was seeing

the buffaloes up close.

     Dad was driving on the first morning of our trip. He yelled, “look

over their!” We saw a herd of buffalo heading our way. All the cars

stoped to let the buffaloes cross. They walked right between our cars.

I could almost touch them. Mom said, What great pictures we’ll have!”

     We saw some bears, foxes and moose later. Did you know that a

moose can weigh up to 1300 pounds. I want to find out more about

these animals.

     We got home yesterday, and I went right to the library. I found a

book called Thunder On the Plains. Dad found one called In the

Company of Moose I can’t wait to read the books!

Your friend John
